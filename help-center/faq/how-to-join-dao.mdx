## Overview

Membership is open to anyone with a compatible crypto wallet.

## Steps to Join

1. Install MetaMask or similar Ethereum wallet.
2. Visit `https://1bz.dao`
3. Connect your wallet via the "Join DAO" button.
4. Approve the connection in your wallet.
5. Register your DAO profile.

<Callout kind="info">
You need ETH for transaction fees.
</Callout>
