## Quick Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Proposals">
    Go to `https://1bz.dao/proposals` and connect your wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Proposal'">
    Select the "New Proposal" button to begin.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out the form">
    Enter title, details, and any required parameters.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit & Approve">
    Submit the proposal. Approve in your wallet when prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>
