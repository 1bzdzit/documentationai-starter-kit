---
title: Cannot Connect Wallet
description: If your wallet won't connect to biznode.
---

## Symptoms

- Wallet connection fails
- No prompt in browser

## Possible Causes

- Browser extension disabled
- Network mismatch (not Ethereum Mainnet)

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Wallet Extension">
    Ensure MetaMask (or your wallet) is active and unlocked.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch to Mainnet">
    Select Ethereum Mainnet in your wallet network options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh and Connect">
    Reload the page and click "Connect Wallet" again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
